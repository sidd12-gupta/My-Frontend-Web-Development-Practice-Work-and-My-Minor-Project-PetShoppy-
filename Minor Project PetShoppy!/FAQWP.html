Frequently Asked Questions!

Does PetShoppy! contain all the accessories for all kinds of animals?
PetShoppy! is limited to the accessories for dogs, cats, birds and some small animals like hamsters,etc. But PetShoppy!, in the future, will also provide accessories for more animals.

Can I feed my puppy adult food?
PetShoppy! doesn't recommend this, as puppies need a lot of things to grow into healthy, happy dogs. Puppies need calcium for bone growth, protein for healthy muscle tissue, and lots of nutrients to allow these fat little balls of fur to grow into healthy adult dogs. Adult food contains nutrients for an adult dog, not puppies.
My pet is overweight, what can I do?
If you feel your dog or cat has put on a few pounds to the point where it's unhealthy, then giving them a light food is a great option. We stock many different brands for both dogs and cats.
Can I feed my cat dog food?
People often ask this question as dog food is usually cheaper than cat food, and many cat owners have seen their cats stealing dog food from their dog's food bowl and wonder whether it's okay for cats to eat. We certainly don't recommend it, cats and dogs both have very different nutritional needs and therefore should have different foods to suit them. A few nibbles from the dog bowl however won't do your cat any harm!
How do I prevent my pet from getting fleas?
Regular flea treatment is important to keep your pet safe from the nasty parasites that are waiting to jump on to your pet during walks and even in your own garden. We stock plenty of flea treatments at PetShoppy! including Frontline, Advantage, Bob Martin, Johnsons and Beaphar.
What is a complete food?
A complete food is a tasty, fully balanced, quality food. It contains all the essential vitamins and minerals to ensure the overall health and condition of your pet.
