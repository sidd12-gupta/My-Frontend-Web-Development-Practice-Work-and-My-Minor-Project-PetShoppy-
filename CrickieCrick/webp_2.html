Ben Stokes - The Journey to The England Test Captain

Benjamin Andrew Stokes is an English Cricketer who is the current captain of the English Test Team and has got an OBE for his services. That's not it, he has gone through alot of struggles in his way to this. Be it the Bristol bar scandal or the Mental Health Issue which he went through in 2021. But they say that you need to go through the struggles in your life to achieve something big. He is truly an inspiration to his journey to the English Test Team Captain alongside with the new coach Brendon McCullum.The best match for Ben Stokes would be the Ashes 2019 Headingley 3rd Test.
